Hey — I'm Schy, a multi-disciplinary creator who lives in the space between making things beautiful and making them work. I'm currently pursuing my studies with a focus on Information Technology, while actively training in Ethical Hacking through EC-Council — because understanding how systems break is how you build ones that don't.

I believe the best digital products are the ones that feel inevitable — where every interaction is smooth, every pixel earns its place, and the underlying system is solid enough to trust. That's what I'm constantly building toward.

When I'm not prototyping in Figma or writing code, you'll find me diving into penetration testing labs, exploring enterprise tools like SAP, or obsessing over why a certain typeface feels right in a layout.

I love working on things that matter — products that solve real problems for real people, with craft and intention at every layer.
